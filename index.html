FAQs

Discover answers to your most pressing questions about Curology and our personalized skincare products.

What is Curology?

Curology is an e-commerce platform that offers personalized skincare solutions. Our products, including tailored moisturizers and cleansers, are designed specifically for your unique skin needs. We believe that skincare should be as individual as you are.

How does it work?

After signing up, you'll complete a brief questionnaire about your skin. Based on your responses, our experts will curate a personalized skincare regimen just for you. This ensures that you receive products that truly cater to your skin's needs.

Are the products safe?

Yes, all Curology products are formulated with safety in mind. We use high-quality ingredients that are dermatologist-tested and suitable for various skin types. Your skin's health is our top priority.

How do I order?

Ordering is simple! After completing your skin assessment, you can choose your products and add them to your cart. Once you're ready, proceed to checkout to finalize your order.

What if I have issues?

If you encounter any issues, our customer support team is here to help. You can reach out via the contact form or email us directly. We're dedicated to ensuring your satisfaction with our products.
